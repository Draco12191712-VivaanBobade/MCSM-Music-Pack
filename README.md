# MCSM-Music-Pack
## Minecraft: Story Mode 2015-2017 Themed Music added to vanilla Minecraft
 This addon brings lots of music from the well-cherished game - Minecraft: Story Mode, to vanilla Minecraft Bedrock! Many of the songs are created by Antimo and Welles, and I am just porting them to Minecraft Bedrock. I do not own any of the rights to the music or the game. Thank you for considering downloading this project. Hope you like it!
